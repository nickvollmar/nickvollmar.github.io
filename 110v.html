Lxii. Lxiii. Signs [for] how you might know whether a person has water-elf-disease and a leechdom against that and a charm to sing on [it] and that same thing a person may sing on wounds.
Lxiiii. Wið deofle liþe drenc 7 ungemynde 7 wiþ deofles costunga.
Lxiiii. Against the devil and insanity17 and against temptations of the devil, a light drink.
