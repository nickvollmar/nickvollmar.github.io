smire þone man mid on þa þunwonge 7 bufan þam eagum 7 ufan þæt heafod 7 þa breost 7 under þam earmum þa sidan þeos sealf is god wiþ ælcre feondes costunga 7 ælf sidenne 7 lenctenadle.
Smear the person with [it] on the temples and above the eyes and on top of the head and the breast and under the arms, the sides. This salve is good against every temptation of the fiend and the influence of an elf and spring disease.
Gif þu wilt lacnian gewits seocne man gedo bydene fulle cealdes wætres dryp þriwa on þæs drences beþe þone man on þam wætre 7 ete se man gehalgodne half 7 cyse garlea 7 cropleac 7 drince þæs drences scenc fulne 7 þonne he sie bebaþþod smire mid þære sealfe swiþe 7 siþþan him sel sie wyrc him þonne wyrc him þonne swiðne drenc utyrnendum.
If you wish to heal a wit-sick person, make a barrel full of cold water. Drip the drink three times in [it]. Warm the person in the water and have the person eat holy bread and cheese, garlic and leek and drink a cup full of the drink and when he is bathed, smear him thoroughly with [that salve] and afterwards he is well. Then make him a strong purgative drink.
Wyrc þus þone drenc nim lybcornes leaf 7 celeþonian moran 7 glædenan moran 7 hocces moran 7 ellenes wyttruman rinde wyl on ealað læt standan neahterne on þa wyrtum ahlyttre þonne 7 gewyrm do buteran to 7 sealt sele drincan.
Make thus the drink: take the leaf of caper spurge fruit and celandine root and yellow iris root and common mallow's root and elder root's bark. Boil in ale. Let stand over night on those herbs. Then purify and warm. Put butter and salt into [it]. Give to drink.
Wyrc spiwe drenc utyrnendne nim feowertig lybcorna berend wel 7 gegnid on nioþowearde celeþonian 7 hocces moran 7 twa clufe þære clufehtan wenwyrte 7 hwerhwette niþewearde an lytel 7 hamwyrte moran med micel gedo ealle þa wyrta swiþe wel clæne 7 gecnua do on eala bewreoh lætstandan neahterne
Make a purgative spew drink. Take forty caper spurge fruits. Peel well and grind [them into] the lower part of celandine and common mallow's root and two cloves of the cloved lesser celandine and a little of the lower part of squirting cucumber and a moderate amount of homewort. Make all the herbs very clean and pound. Put in ale. Boil. Let stand over night.
