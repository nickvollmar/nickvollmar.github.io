If you wish to cure that person, consider his bearing and be aware of which sex he is. If it is a man and he looks up when you first look at him and his face is yellow-black, you might cure that man perfectly if he is not therein too long35. If it is a woman and she looks down when you first look at her and her face is dark red, that you might also cure. If it is on [the person] a day's time longer than twelve months and his visage is such, then you might better him for a while and might not perfectly cure him.
