xLi. Make a good drink against all temptations of the devil. Take betony, bishopwort, lupin, corncockle, fumitory, fuller's teasel, yarrow. Lay [them] under an altar. Sing nine masses over [them]. Make a good salve against the temptation of the devil. Bishopwort, lupin, viper's bugloss, strawberry stalk, the cloved lesser celandine, eorðrima, bramble apple, pennyroyal, wormwood. Pound all the herbs. Boil in good butter. Wring through a cloth. Set under an altar. Sing nine masses over [it].
